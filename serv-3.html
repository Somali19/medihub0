AUDIO TRANSCRIPTION


  • MediHub hosts accomplished scribes who are well equipped in providing the service of audio transcription to doctors. Audio transcription comprises the scribe taking direct dictations and instructions from the physicians regarding what to include in a patient's EHR. Having collected all the required data, the scribe prepares a well-detailed report and presents the completed document to the physician within 16 hours.
  • Our entire audio transcription programme has turned out to be quite flexible and budget friendly. All you require is just a mobile telephone. Yes it is, in fact, that convenient and easy! Our scribes are well trained to sincerely capture key information.
  • It doesn't stop there. Our scribes record the key information and document it very accurately and precisely in the EHR of the patient. They work meticulously on sorting the patient's direct medical reports from the encounter into the apt EHR field.
  • Our highly trained, in-house scribes work very hard to uphold the standard working qualities that we preach. You can totally rely on us when it comes to making heightened EHR reports for the patients at the press of a button. Our motto is to enable health providers to perform at the top of their ability, without having to focus on the paperwork.
