ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE ?

  • MediHub is a startup company.
  • We act as scribers for doctors, nurses, physicians, clinicians, etc and perform our duties in a HIPAA compliant environment.
  • It is our pride to house extremely well-trained, professional, and experienced scribers.
  • Our well-organized team has been trained to adapt themselves to the individual choices and preferences of each and every healthcare provider.
  • We are also ready to assist in cases of critical care, managing communications as well as referrals.
  • Currently, we provide our services from India, with our office based in Andhra Pradesh. Our headquarters are in Atlanta, United States.

WHAT WE DO ?

  • We provide highly professional medical scribes carefully matched to specific needs.
  • Our scribes receive training specific to your specialty and meticulously document each visit.
  • At ScribePlus we strongly stick to the values of security, confidentiality, and integrity.
  • Our well-trained scribes make sure that the health information of the patient is not disclosed in accordance with our adherence to HIPAA.
  • Our medical scribes specialize in unloading the provider's burden of medical data collection and data entry into the EHR, resulting in improved operational workflow, department metrics and a return of the provider to the patient's bedside.

OUR MISSION

  • Being aware of the already crushing workload of the physicians, our company's mission is to help them relax by taking up this crucial yet meticulous job.
  • Our conscientious documentation of the EHR will increase the doctor's time to focus on the diagnosis of the patients.
  • This not only aids the physician to increase the number of patient visits leading to more revenue for them but also lengthens their time for family, relaxation, and recreation.

OUR VISION

  • Our motto is to ease the work of the physician to a great extent through all these means.
  • Locating information to be reviewed, history of allergies, immunization, health maintenance, research data, patient instructions; we are ready to provide skilled services in all these spectrums!
  • By working hard for our clients, and innovating to meet the growing demands of healthcare, we hope to expand the scribe industry and fundamentally improve the delivery of care for medical practitioners worldwide.
