VIRTUAL LIVE SCRIBING


  • MediHub offers you the live-scribing service with a heightened "in-room" experience. To ensure an enhanced and improved patient-doctor diagnosis, our scribes will review a patient's medical charts beforehand. This will keep the physician updated with the detailed medical report of the individual, from an earlier visit.
  • The scribe listens in very carefully when the doctor is diagnosing the patient. With proper attention to every detail, the scribe incorporates the specifics of the patient's encounter in the report they prepares. The scribes will always be ready to step in and interact with the patients according to the doctor's preference. It is the duty of the scribe to chart the detailed reports of the patient straight from the examination room into the existing Electronic Health Record of the patient within a mere 3 minutes.
  • Our live-scribing service provides the additional benefit to the physician of referring to the priorly documented EHR of the patients. Their performance is made more convenient and lucid through interaction with our scribes in real-time.
  • Reviewing and signing off of the documentation is effectively finished well before seeing the next patient. Our live-scribing service is not only easy but also very secure. All these factors come together to make our live-scribing service a very efficient method of enhancing the physician's performance as well as reducing time. It is important to mention that our live scribing services benefit not only the healthcare providers but also the patients.
